Worried about notification noise?

I get it. Open the group → tap its name at the top → Notifications → Mute. Or long-press the group in your chat list → Mute.

You can mute it forever and check in when you feel like it. That’s how most members use it.

How the group works

The group is organized into Topics. They’re tabs inside the group, a bit like channels, so conversations stay tidy.

To reply to one specific message, long-press it, or swipe it, and choose Reply.

House rules

  • Be kind and neighborly.
  • Keep it Burlington-relevant.
  • No spam or self-promo without asking.
  • Disagree about restaurants, not about people.
