Find an Apartment in Burlington

The best listings in this town never make it to Zillow — they go to whoever emailed the property manager first. Here is every manager worth emailing, then everywhere else worth checking.

The move that actually works: pick the managers below whose buildings fit you, and send each a short email — what you need (beds, budget, move-in month), that you have income docs and references ready, and a phone number. You'll hear about units before they're listed anywhere. Most Burlington leases turn over June 1; the winter lull (Nov–Feb) is when managers are most willing to deal.
